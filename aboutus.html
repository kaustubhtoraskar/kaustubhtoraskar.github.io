ABOUT US

VPS Techub Private Limited is a start-up company. It was established in 2016 by three engineering students. The company has developed an Aadhaar based hospital management system. The integration of Aadhaar with healthcare in India is the fuel that runs VPS Techub. Our Start-up strongly support the idea of creating a “Digital India”. We want to contribute towards the making of Digital India through our product. Our product, VPS Hospital Management System will pave way towards the digitisation of the entire healthcare industry in India. The USP of our system is that it makes use of Aadhaar. We are also proud members of Sardar Patel Institute of Technology’s Technical Business Incubation Centre. As a start-up, we thrive to optimise on all resources made available to us. We at VPS Techub truly believe that “INCUBATION” is the force which accelerates the progress of a start-up. We highly recommend it to all start-ups.
