Medical Management Tools

India is a home to 1.252 billion population. The medical data associated with this huge population is humongous. And the management of this medical data using traditional means is cumbersome. It is time to prioritize health. Digitising medical records is the need of the hour. VPS TecHub provides the simplest solution. The VPS TecHub’s Medical Management Tools – VPS Hospital Management System (HMS) and VPS Clinical Management System (CMS).

VPS HMS & CMS


VPS TecHub’s HMS and CMS manage everything from patient registration to updating medical records to billing. All these processes are initiated with the use of Aadhaar card. This is how it works.


Hospital & Clinic end Client end
Patient produces his/her Aadhar card at the registration counters of hospitals. Mobile application used by patient to register at a hospital.
Aadhar card scanned, personal details of the patient retrieved onto our HMS in less than 20 seconds. Application generates a Q.R code.
For a recurring patient, personal details combined with medical data associated with the patient. Q.R code scanned at the respective hospital.
Registration and expense sheet printed for the patient. Personal and Medical data of the patient retrieved by HMS.





How it works

Your Adhaar card is produced at the registration desk.

QR code of your Adhaar card is scanned.

The person gets registered in hospital.
