Organ Donation

"1 ORGAN DONOR CAN SAVE 8 LIVES. 1 ORGAN DONOR CAN BETTER THE LIVES OF 50 PEOPLE."

Organ Donation is truly the best last service a person can do for others. Millions suffer from ailments that can be cured through organ transplant. The problem is not the actual procedure but what comes before it. There is very little awareness among people regarding organ donation. Several misconceptions govern people’s decision on the issue of organ donation.

WE AT VPS TECHUB UNDERSTAND THIS.


VPS TecHub has taken an initiative towards organ donation. It has developed a software for registration of organ donors. In India, there are several NGOs that support and work for organ donation like MOHAN Foundation , ZTCC , Narmada Kidney Foundation , etc. VPS TecHub in collaboration with MOHAN Foundation organised an Organ Donation Awareness Campaign in Sardar Patel Institute of Technology in February 2017.


During this campaign, professionals from MOHAN Foundation educated a large audience on organ donation. They cleared all doubts and possible misconceptions that people have regarding organ donation. 41 people registered as organ donors during this campaign. Know more. (link to blog – organ donation campaign)


The registration for organ donors was guided by MOHAN Foundation and was done using VPS TecHub’s Organ Donation Software. This software has been developed as per the standards set by NOTTO (National Organ and Tissue Transplant Organisation). All legalities were observed.


The registration for organ donors was done using their Aadhaar cards. The Q.R code on their Aadhaar card was scanned to retrieve basic details in record time (20 seconds). All other pertinent information was then manually filled up in the digital form generated by the software. MOHAN foundation issued donor cards to the people who registered.
